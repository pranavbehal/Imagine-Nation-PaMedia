    • To Parents

    We know that as a parent, you want the best for your child. That's why we've created a TV show that is not only entertaining, but also educational and safe for kids of all ages.

    Imagine Nation is rated TV Y, which means it is appropriate for children of all ages. Our show is designed to be interactive, engaging, and educational. We strive to provide a fun and safe space for kids to learn, grow, and use their imagination.

    In Imagine Nation, your child will follow the adventures of four friends: Lily, Max, Emma, and Tyler. Each character brings their own unique personality and skills to the group, making for a well-rounded and dynamic team. Your child will be able to learn valuable lessons and values from these characters, such as kindness, curiosity, and problem-solving skills.

    In addition to the TV show, our website also offers an interactive area where kids can play games, solve puzzles, and learn new things. This is the perfect way to keep your child's mind active and engaged.

    Q&A:

    Where can I watch Imagine Nation, and when is it live?
    You can watch Imagine Nation everyday from 3:00 PM to 4:00 PM on the ABC123 Network.
    Is Imagine Nation safe for my child?
    Yes, Imagine Nation is a safe and fun space for children to learn and grow. We carefully design our show and website to be appropriate for kids of all ages.
    Which age group is Imagine Nation for?
    Although Imagine Nation is suitable for children of all ages, we specifically tailor some of the content towards children between 4 and 8 years old.
    What does Imagine Nation teach children?
    Imagine Nation teaches children valuable lessons and skills, such as teamwork, problem-solving, and creativity. In addition, we also teach children about the importance of kindness and respect. Imagine Nation also reviews some basic math, English, and science concepts.
